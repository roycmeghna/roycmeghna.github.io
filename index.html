PROJECTS & PUBLICATION

A state-of-the-art and accurate method to predict depth of anesthesia by deep neural networks via ECG and PPG signals.The accuracy achieved is 86%. This work is published in the journal Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, Vol. 68, 102663, pp. 1-12 (2021).


Gesture Vocaliser for Deaf & Mute

An inexpensive gloved technology which eases the communication between the disabled by converting the hand action of the wearer into text using Arduino.
